About Me

Dr. Claudia Khalil is a passionate and highly motivated dentist, recently graduated with a Doctorate in Dental Surgery (DDS). She has been working in the field for the past 6 months, providing high-quality dental care and focusing on patient comfort and satisfaction.

Education & Training

Dr. Khalil graduated at the top of her class from Lebanese University LU, where she received her DDS. During her studies, she completed several internships and hands-on training sessions in both general and cosmetic dentistry, gaining valuable experience in dental procedures and patient care.

My Approach

As a fresh graduate, Dr. Khalil brings a modern, cutting-edge approach to dentistry, utilizing the latest techniques and technologies. She is dedicated to helping patients achieve their healthiest, most confident smiles by combining innovative treatments with personalized care.

Continuing Education

Dr. Khalil is committed to continually enhancing her skills through workshops and seminars in the latest dental innovations. She is currently expanding her expertise in cosmetic and restorative dentistry.

Outside the Office

Outside of her dental practice, Dr. Khalil enjoys staying active, reading about advances in dental technology, and contributing to dental health outreach programs in her community.

General Dentistry

Providing routine care such as cleanings, fillings, and preventive treatment to keep your teeth healthy.

Cosmetic Dentistry

Offering teeth whitening, veneers, and other procedures to enhance the appearance of your smile.

Oral Surgery

Expert surgical care for wisdom tooth extraction, implants, and other dental procedures.
